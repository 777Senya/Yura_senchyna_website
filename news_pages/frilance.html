For the first time in Ukraine, the company can work with any non-cash freelancers

The leading freelance platform in Ukraine Freelancehunt.com introduced a new service for the cooperation of legal persons and freelancers. "Safe Business" will allow for payment by bank transfer and receive closing documents for accounting in paper or electronic form - depending on customer needs.

Tools for remote collaboration allow to hire performers from across the country and get the best deals as compared to local contractors. The designer from Lviv, Kharkiv programmer, a translator from Odessa - they are all just a click away, and now working with them is even better for business. Service not only provides the tools for comfortable work, but also allows you to receive closing documents for accounting. The package of documents includes a standard set of primary documents - a contract, invoice and certificate of completion.


Even if the contractor - not the FLP


The new service allows you to take orders from freelancers paying projects with a checking account and receive closing documents, even if the final performer is not a legal entity or individual entrepreneur. Freelancehunt mediates in every transaction, and if the final performer is not a businessman, self-pay taxes for it according to the current legislation. If the perpetrator - a legal entity or FLP, the taxes he pays himself.


Innovative services - the ability to use electronic document management


Another advantage of the new service - the integration of "Safe Business" from Freelancehunt.com and "Papka24" by Privatbank. "Papka24" - an online service that allows businesses and individuals to sign documents with contractors in electronic form is safe and perfectly legal. If one of the parties still need to receive the documents in paper form - Freelancehunt provide them, allowing the other side to work with the document in the "Papka24".


Even today, "Safe Business" appreciated companies operating in various fields and directions. Attracted in their projects designers, programmers, marketers and other freelancers working for Freelancehunt.com confirm that the use of remote experts - is the perfect solution for a rational distribution of the company's budget and reduce business costs.


Freelancehunt.com - the largest in Ukraine freelance market, operating in the market for ten years. At the service has more than 200,000 freelancers and customers from across the country, as well as near and far abroad. At the service have the tools to secure cooperation (cooperation with redundant verification of identity), service competition, the ability to search for artists rated in different categories. Among the performers are representatives of dozens of specialties, such as design, programming, online marketing, architecture, engineering, law, translation of texts.
