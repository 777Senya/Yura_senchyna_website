Kiev programmer wrote a plugin that blocks in Facebook posts about the elections and Trampe

No news feed, no belt on Facebook or Twitter has not done today without mentioning the US elections and the victory of Donald Trump. While one part of the audience actively shares his expert assessment of the consequences of such a choice, the other irritated ubiquitous posts on the topic.

For such cases, Kiev programmer Max Fry wrote a plugin for Chrome, which locks in Facebook posts about the election. If you download and activate a plug-in, instead of verbal battles about the fate of America, you will see the dice with the sour face of the future president.

"Often, after some infopovoda Feed Facebook zaspamlivaetsya bunch of duplicate jokes, and therefore it becomes impossible to read. I have a running time, which is able to filter such content, "- says the developer.


Its plug-in identifies and blocks positions on the content (links and text), assessing them on stop words, so do not get rid of the fotozhab. It works only in the web version of Facebook, for the Ukrainian, Russian and English languages.


Previously, the developer laid out such decisions to block advertisements and drawings in "VKontakte".
