"Ukrzaliznytsia" has launched a mobile version of the online sale of tickets

Buy e-tickets on the train with a smartphone, clearly more convenient than going to the ticket office and stand in line. "Ukrzaliznitsya" has decided to simplify the passengers a little more life and launched a mobile version of its website Ticket. Promise and a separate application, but later.

According to the head of state enterprise board of Wojciech Balchuna, now about 30% of the tickets for the train are sold through the Internet, with most of the sales of tickets online falls on the railway site.

According to the head of the enterprise, this version adapted for all major mobile platforms. "We hope this is another incentive for users to apply to our website, to get rid of the queues and reduce the burden on the cash register," - said Balchun.

Recall, the international online service for ordering tickets on the buses Busfor raised $ 20 million from the Russian Direct Investment Fund "Baring Vostok" and "Elbrus Capital." The round also took part in the Moscow InVenture Partners fund, which has previously invested in the company.
