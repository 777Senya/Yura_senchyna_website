Instagram updated Stories and is working on the launch of live broadcasts

Owned photographic Facebook social network is working on the launch of live video in an application. This Instagram CEO Kevin Systrom said in an interview with the Financial Times. Also today in the application is updated section Stories, where users will be able to celebrate and create a Boomerang-looped video.

"Live events are really interesting for us. I think they can leverage what we do. If I try to strengthen relations with those whom I love, then view its live video - a wonderful way to get closer to him, "- said Sistrom edition.


Руководитель фотоприложения не предоставил никаких деталей о сроках запуска или том, как функция будет реализована. Однако несколько недель назад издание TJournal писало о тестировании новой возможности одним из читателей. Согласно скриншотам, которыми он поделился, прямые трансляции будут отображаться вверху основного экрана среди Stories. Они будут отмечаться красной надписью «Live».

Instagram has added a short video Stories, which disappear within 24 hours, by analogy with Snapchat, in August this year. From today they were able to add links, and add users to mark looped video Boomerang leave the main application unnecessarily. Reference is only available for verified users, you can have the rest of the novelties to try out the latest version of the application.Links to external resources, which until now was not possible to place anywhere, except to describe the profile will open when you click on the button at the bottom See More stories. Opens they will in the built-in browser Instagram.
