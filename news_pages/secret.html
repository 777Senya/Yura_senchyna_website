Developers revive anonymous communication service Secret

Secret Service secrets of anonymous publication has gained popularity in the leakage of information from large IT-companies and the media. In May 2014, he has started in many countries, including Ukraine, but then closed in April 2015. However, its founders before it managed to sell part of the shares and get everyone on the $ 3 million. Now they want to run the Secret 2.0.

One of its co-founder and CEO David Baitau recently said on Twitter, that the second version of the application is returned, "it is too important to not exist." But, according to founder before restarting Secret, he must make sure that the community will support it. "This is another application that is not profit-oriented", - he said


From David ribbons on social networks, it is clear that he was not happy about the victory of Donald Trump in the presidential elections, and the elections that it made me think about the revival of an application. "What a pity that there was no Secret on this (election - Ed.) Week. Especially before Tuesday. So that I can better understand what is actually felt by the people "- he wrote earlier.


It is not clear when the next version of the app will be released. It is known that Baitau not want this time to raise money from investors (the last Secret has raised $ 35 million).
