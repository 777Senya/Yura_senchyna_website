Starbucks chose the Ukrainian design for Christmas decoration of their cups

It's time to be proud of their Ukrainian startups origin, drinking coffee Christmas in San Francisco. Ukrainka Anna Bucciarelli (Shinlova) has created a Christmas design for Starbucks cups with elements Petrykivka mural that organically fit into the traditional Christmas image of Santa, trees, houses and deer. Cups with traditional Ukrainian patterns appear on the iconic Starbucks Red Cups in coffee shops around the world.

"Drumroll! My design for Starbucks is now available in 75 countries around the world ", - wrote Anna to Facebook.


Every year on the eve of New Year holidays Starbucks starts to sell coffee cups festive red instead of the traditional green. Due to the exclusivity of the design, many customers network collect the cups. Anne Design and 12 artists from around the world chose the Starbucks in the competition of more than 1200 works from 13 countries.


Together with other winners from Canada, Indonesia, UAE, South Korea, Russia and the United States, Anna was invited to New York to note preparation for Christmas at Starbucks with the head of Howard Schultz. Designers will participate in the official launch of the updated type cups unofficial mecca of all startups and hipsters. The process Anna has promised to cover in his Facebook.


Anna was born in Ukraine, which at that time was part of the USSR. He moved to Canada and graduated in Toronto, where he lives today. The artist specializes in the design based on traditional Ukrainian Petrykivka painting, as stated on its website portfolio.
