Trump wins - Immigration Canada website falls

Many today woke up and did not understand what was happening. But the Americans following election all night, as the vote count more feared for the future of their country and for their own. These concerns resulted in a sharp increase in interest in the northern neighbor the United States. State website on emigration to Canada could not stand the load of about 22:30 EST (5:30 in Kiev) and still remains inaccessible.

Interest in Canada also confirms the increasing number of expatriates on Google searches.

The publication Business Insider claims that one of the most visited articles on the site was "How to move to Canada and become a citizen." US northern neighbor has a sufficiently clear emigration policy, and will be particularly easy to move and highly skilled entrepreneurs.
