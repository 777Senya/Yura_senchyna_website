The 'Intertelekom' confiscated equipment. More than a million subscribers were left without communication

On Friday evening the Ukrainian Security Service raided the office in Odessa major Ukrainian telecom provider 'Intertelecom'. During the search were confiscated equipment, which is why no communication left more than 1 million subscribers throughout Ukraine.

As it became known AIN.UA from an anonymous source, the security forces have disconnected the router through which all traffic to the subscribers' Intertelekoma. Withdrawal continues: take away computer equipment and personal staff. Lawyers in the office is not allowed, saying that a special operation carried out. According to the source, the removal is carried out in Odessa employees of the Main Directorate of the Security Service.


He said that the investigative actions in a company no one interferes. "We have provided security personnel and investigators all the necessary materials in order to remove the suspicion of the crimes that we are charged with. We provided full access to the experts who have come together with representatives of the security services - to all the systems, all the files to all servers, all consoles ... ", - he said, adding that the investigators and experts to ensure that the charges were groundless, however, still we made the decision to withdraw the important equipment that will disable the network throughout Ukraine.


In an interview with "reporter" Akulov appealed to the President with a request to contribute to ensure that the security forces did not violate the law and do not impede the work of the company. Recall, 8 September the Verkhovna Rada in the first reading forbidden to seize servers and any other equipment during a search. However, the bill is finally adopted.
