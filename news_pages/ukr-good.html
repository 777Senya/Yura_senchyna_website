Ukrainians have created a iOS-app that helps to develop good habits

We recently wrote about how to develop good habits and why it matters. Developers from Odessa decided to facilitate this task and created a Daily Habits app for iOS and Apple Watch, which helps to develop and maintain good habits and get rid of the bad ones. The "base set" free app, the developers plan to make on a paid pro-version.

The application works on the principle of opening up new "achivok" - it is possible to set goals and track progress of their achievements. His success can be shared with friends and family in special groups, where you can invite people with the same interests.


If you do not know where to start, in the Daily Habits has a built-in "library habit" from which you can choose something useful. Habits, on which work is finished, you can back up.

Create an application Odessa company XwaveSoft, which specializes in developing mobile applications for iOS platforms, and Mac OS X. He founded it Yevenko Denis in 2009. The main direction of the company - the development of applications to improve productivity, organization and time-saving. One of the most popular products of the studio was Be Focused application (the original name of Pomodoro Time). Daily Habits, which also solves the problem of personal effectiveness, has become a logical continuation of the line of existing products XwaveSoft.
